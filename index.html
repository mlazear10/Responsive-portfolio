About Me

My name is Michael Lazear and I am an Orange County native. My interests include yoga, cycling, golf, coding, finance, and zen. I went to school at UCI for my undergraduate degree, ZOT ZOT! My favorite TV shows currently are Silicon Valley, The Last Kingdom, and Shameless. I can relate to certain aspects of shameless. I am 28 years young and try to live each day as it is a journey.

My favorite fruits are mangoes and apples and I like the color green. In Zen, the enso is a circle that is hand-drawn in one or two uninhibited brushstrokes to express a moment when the mind is free to let the body create. I like this symbol and it is my favicon. The current zen book I am reading is "Moon in a Dew Drop". Zen Master Dogen recommends to do zazen (meditation) and endeavor in the way, depending on the teaching of the buddha ancestors, under the guidance of a teacher, without distinguishing between beginning or advanced, and without being concerned with the ordinary or sacred.
